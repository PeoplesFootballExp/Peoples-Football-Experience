
# Attributes

This page will outline all the attributes of a player and how each will affect the player's feel in the game. These attributes will be present for every player, regardless of position. This allows any player to technically play as any position on the field. 

# Technical Ability

The following attributes detail how well a player executes actions involving the ball. 


| Attribute   | General Function                                                                |
| ----------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Passing     | Accuracy and weight of ground passes                                            |
| Crossing    | Accuracy and curve of lofted passes into the box                                |
| Dribbling   | Ability to run with the ball while maintaining control                          |
| First Touch | How cleanly a player controls the ball when receiving a pass                    |
| Finishing   | Accuracy of shots on goal (excluding long shots)                                |
| Power       | Power of shots on goal (excluding long shots)                                   |
| Long Shots  | Accuracy and power of shots taken outside the penalty box                       |
| Tackling    | Effectiveness and cleanliness of attempts to win the ball from an opponent      |
| Marking     | Ability to stay close to an opponent and limit their space and options          |
| Heading     | Skill in directing the ball with their head (both attacking and defensive)      |
| Technique   | General proficiency and comfort with complex technical moves; execution quality |
| Free Kicks  | Accuracy, curve, and power on direct and indirect free kicks                    |
| Corners     | Accuracy and curve on corner kicks                                              |
| Penalties   | Composure and Accuracy when taking penalty kicks                                |
| Long Throws | Distance and accuracy of long-distance throws-ins                               |


# Mental Ability


| Attribute     | General Function                                                                                                        |
| ------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Anticipation  | Ability to read the game and predict where the ball/players will go next                                                |
| Positioning   | Ability to find the correct defensive position relative to the ball, opponent, and goal                                 |
| Off the Ball  | Ability to find good attacking space when not in possession of the ball                                                 |
| Decisions     | Speed and correctness of choosing the bets course of action during play                                                 |
| Composure     | Ability to maintain technique and focus under pressure (especially in the final third both offensively and defensively) |
| Concentration | Consistency of mental focus throughout the match (avoiding errors)                                                      |
| Vision        | Ability to see potential passing options and chances others miss                                                        |
| Teamwork      | Willingness to stick to tactical instructions and work for the team                                                     |
| Aggression    | Propensity to contest balls vigorously and commit to tackles                                                            |
| Determination | Desire to win and bounce back from setbacks during a game                                                               |
| Flair         | Tendency and ability to execute unpredictable, creative, and risky actions                                              |
| Work Rate     | Effort and intensity applied throughout the match                                                                       |
| Bravery       | Willingness to risk injury or physical contact for the team (e.g blocking shots)                                        |
| Leadership    | Ability to influence and motivate teammates on the field                                                                |

# Physical Ability


| Attribute       | General Function                                                             |
| --------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Pace            | Maximum speed reached while running without the ball                         |
| Acceleration    | How quickly a player reaches their top speed from a standstill               |
| Agility         | Ease and speed of changing direction and twisting                            |
| Balance         | Ability to remain steady on their feet when dribbling or being tackled       |
| Strength        | Physical power used to win contested balls or shield the ball from opponents |
| Jumping Reach   | Maximum height a player can reach with their head when jumping               |
| Stamina         | Ability to maintain physical effort throughout the match without fatigue     |
| Natural Fitness | Rate of recovery between matches and resistance to losing condition          |
| Height          | Player height (in cm) - influences aerial duels                              |
| Weight          | Player weight (in kg) - influences strength contests and balance             |

# Goal Keepers Specifics


| Attribute          | General Function                                                                            |
| ------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Handling           | Security when catching or holding the ball                                                  |
| Reflexes           | Speed of reaction to close-range or powerful shots                                          |
| Ones-on-ones       | Effectiveness in saving when a forward is clean through on goal                             |
| Aerial Reach       | Ability to claim high crosses and corner kicks                                              |
| Kicking            | Accuracy and distance of distribution with the feet                                         |
| Throwing           | Accuracy and distance of distribution with the hands                                        |
| Keeper Positioning | Ability to position themselves correctly to cover the goal against shots                    |
| Command of Area    | Authority and effectiveness in organizing the defense and claiming balls within the box     |
| Eccentricity       | Tendency to take ricks or engage in unconventional actions (e.g. rushing out aggressively ) |
